How to Get a Free Domain Name in 2026 — 7 Proven Methods

Published: May 1, 2026 · 8 min read · OnlyFreeDomain Team

Getting a free domain name in 2026 is completely possible — you just need to know where to look. This guide covers every legitimate method.

Method 1: Get a Free Domain With a Hosting Plan

The most reliable way to get a free domain is to sign up for a web hosting plan. Several top registrars including Hostinger, Bluehost, and DreamHost include a free .com domain for the first year with any hosting purchase.

Method 2: Use a Free TLD Extension

Freenom offers completely free domain extensions including .tk, .ml, .ga, .cf, and .gq. These are real domain names you can use for personal projects or testing ideas.

Method 3: First-Year Promotional Offers

Major registrars like Namecheap and GoDaddy regularly offer .com domains for $0.99 or even free for the first year as promotional deals for new customers.

Method 4: GitHub Pages Free Subdomain

GitHub Pages gives you a free yourname.github.io subdomain with free hosting included. Ideal for developers and portfolio sites.

Method 5: WordPress.com and Blogger Subdomains

WordPress.com offers free yoursite.wordpress.com subdomains, and Blogger gives you yoursite.blogspot.com completely free with no setup required.

Method 6: Expired Domain Auctions

Thousands of domains expire every day and become available at standard registration price. Tools like ExpiredDomains.net and GoDaddy Auctions can help you find valuable expired domains cheaply.

Method 7: Student and Nonprofit Programs

The GitHub Student Developer Pack includes a free .me domain. Several registrars also offer free .org domains for verified nonprofits.

Which Free Domain Method Is Best for You?

  • Starting a business website? → Get a hosting plan that includes a free .com domain (Hostinger recommended)
  • Just testing an idea? → Use a free .tk or .ml domain from Freenom
  • Building a portfolio as a developer? → GitHub Pages with .github.io subdomain
  • Starting a personal blog? → WordPress.com or Blogger free subdomain
  • Student? → GitHub Student Pack for a free .me domain

Bottom Line: The absolute best way to get a free domain in 2026 is to bundle it with a hosting plan from Hostinger or Bluehost. You get a free .com, professional email, and fast hosting all together.
