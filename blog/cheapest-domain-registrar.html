Hidden Costs to Watch Out For

  • WHOIS Privacy: GoDaddy and Google Domains charge extra. IONOS, Cloudflare, and Namecheap include it free.
  • Renewal vs Registration Price: Always check the renewal price before buying. GoDaddy's renewal is often double the registration price.
  • Transfer Fees: Moving a domain usually costs one year's registration fee. Most good registrars charge the standard rate.
  • Auto-renewal: Enable auto-renewal to prevent accidental domain expiry and potential hijacking.

Our Recommendation

For pure value: IONOS is the clear winner. For renewals: Cloudflare cannot be beaten. For beginners: Namecheap's interface and support are unmatched. Avoid: GoDaddy for long-term domain ownership due to high renewal prices.
